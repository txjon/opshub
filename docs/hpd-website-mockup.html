Services

Decoration

Screen Printing

Up to 8 colors, water-based and plastisol inks. Specialty options: puff, metallic, discharge, high-density. Minimums as low as 48 units.

Embroidery

Flat and 3D puff embroidery. Hats, polos, jackets, patches. Digitizing included.

Full-color, photo-quality prints. Ideal for small runs, complex art, and all-over prints.
